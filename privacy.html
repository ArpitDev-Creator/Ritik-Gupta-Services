Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ritik Gupta ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and handles your information when you visit this website or engage my services.

When you contact me through this website or via email, I may collect the following information:

  • Project Information: Details about your project, requirements, and any content you share with me for the purpose of completing work.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics data such as pages visited and time spent on the site (if analytics tools are used). This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.

2. How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and communicate with you about projects.

When you email me at buildwithdevritik@gmail.com, your email and any information you provide will be stored in my email inbox for the purpose of communication and project management. I will only use your email to respond to you and discuss your project.

Your information is stored securely. I take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

I retain your information only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

5. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Basic session or preference cookies may be used to ensure the website functions correctly.

This website may contain links to external platforms (such as Netlify-hosted project demos). I am not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. I encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me at buildwithdevritik@gmail.com.
