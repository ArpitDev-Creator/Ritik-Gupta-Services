Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a website cost?
Pricing depends on what you need — type of website, number of pages, features, and complexity. I discuss all requirements first and then give a fair, transparent quote. There are no hidden charges.
How long does it take to build a website?
A simple business website typically takes 3–7 days. A more complex web app or multi-page project can take 1–3 weeks. I always give a realistic timeline upfront and stick to it.
Do you provide source code?
Yes! All custom projects include full source code delivery. I also sell source code of my existing apps separately — just ask.
Can you revamp my existing website?
Absolutely. If your current website looks outdated or isn't converting visitors, I can redesign and rebuild it from scratch with a modern, premium look.
How do we communicate during the project?
Primarily via email. I provide regular updates throughout the project so you always know what's happening. You're never left in the dark.
