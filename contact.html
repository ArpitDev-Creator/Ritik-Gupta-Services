How much does a website cost?
Pricing depends on the type of website, number of pages, and features. I discuss all requirements first and give a fair, transparent quote. No hidden charges.
How long does it take to build a website?
A simple business website takes 3–7 days. A complex web app can take 1–3 weeks. I always give a realistic timeline and stick to it.
Do you provide source code?
Yes! All custom projects include full source code delivery. I also sell source code of existing apps separately.
Can you revamp my existing website?
Absolutely. If your current website looks outdated or isn't converting, I can redesign and rebuild it with a modern, premium look.
How do we communicate during the project?
Primarily via email. I provide regular updates so you always know what's happening — you're never left in the dark.
